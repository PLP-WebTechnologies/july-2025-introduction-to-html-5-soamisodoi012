Getting Started with Sustainable Gardening

Published on November 15, 2023

Why Choose Sustainable Gardening?

Sustainable gardening helps protect the environment while creating beautiful outdoor spaces. It focuses on:

  • Water conservation
  • Organic pest control
  • Native plant cultivation
  • Soil health improvement

5 Beginner Tips

  1. Start small with a few easy-to-grow plants
  2. Compost kitchen scraps for natural fertilizer
  3. Collect rainwater for irrigation
  4. Choose native plants adapted to your area
  5. Practice crop rotation in vegetable gardens
